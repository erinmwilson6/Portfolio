About Me

I am currently completing an MSc in Geographic Data Science at the University of Liverpool. My work sits between geography, environmental data, GIS and visual communication.

Background

Geography, Data And Place

I am interested in how spatial data can be used to understand environmental problems more clearly. My projects have explored flood risk, greenspace access, location suitability, natural hazards and climate related questions.

I enjoy work that combines analysis with communication. For me, a strong GIS project is not only technically correct, but also visually clear and easy to interpret.

Technical Areas

Skills

GIS And Mapping

QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, cartography, web mapping, spatial joins, raster and vector analysis.

Programming

Python, R, SQL, Jupyter Notebook, Quarto, data cleaning and reproducible workflows.

Analysis

Spatial statistics, regression, accessibility analysis, suitability analysis, clustering and classification.

Communication

Technical reports, visual storytelling, project summaries, map design and presentation outputs.

Dissertation
