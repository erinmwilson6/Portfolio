Location Analysis

Retail Location Suitability For Asda Express

This project assesses three possible Asda Express locations in Liverpool: Long Lane in Norris Green, Innovation Boulevard in Wavertree and Lark Lane in Aigburth. The study combines demographic, behavioural and retail competition indicators to compare each site.

Research Question

Which of the three proposed Liverpool locations offers the strongest spatial evidence for a new Asda Express?

This project assesses three possible Asda Express locations in Liverpool: Long Lane in Norris Green, Innovation Boulevard in Wavertree and Lark Lane in Aigburth. The study combines demographic, behavioural and retail competition indicators to compare each site.

Data

  • Index of Multiple Deprivation
  • Internet User Classification
  • Daytime and night time population
  • GEOLYTIX retail points
  • Liverpool LSOA boundaries
  • Proposed store locations

Method

  • Created walking catchments around each proposed site
  • Joined deprivation, population and digital behaviour data to LSOA boundaries
  • Assessed convenience store competition within each catchment
  • Mapped site context, deprivation, digital behaviour, population and retail competition
  • Combined the evidence in a weighted scoring matrix

Output

Outcome

The final recommendation identified Long Lane as the strongest location. It had a balanced population profile, high local reliance on physical retail and no competing convenience stores within the walking catchment.
